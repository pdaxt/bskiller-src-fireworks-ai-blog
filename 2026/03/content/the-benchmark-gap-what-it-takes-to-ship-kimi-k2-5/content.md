# The Benchmark Gap: What It Takes to Ship Kimi K2.5

Source: [Fireworks AI Blog](https://fireworks.ai/blog/quality-first-with-kimi-k2p5)

---

Quality is the most important characteristic of a frontier model, and we at Fireworks are committed to getting this right. We’ve written about our GPT-OSS quality efforts here where we fixed several quality issues. This blog is about our experience ensuring quality with Kimi K2.5 - a new SOTA multimodal model from the Moonshot team.

We cover our quality control process, benchmark scores we measured, some interesting bugs we found soon after launch, and how we’re strengthening our process for all future model deployments.
